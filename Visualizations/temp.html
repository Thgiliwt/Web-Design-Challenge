Summary: Latitude vs.Max Temperature

This is a relatively perfect match to the reality as from the earth equator at 0 degree of latitude (or say -23.43D to +23.43D are the tropic zones) , the temperature drops down to either north and south. If we change the xlabel to the absolute values, it is clearly to say that as the latitude increases, the temperature will drop down. Hence it will show a strong correlation between these two factors.
